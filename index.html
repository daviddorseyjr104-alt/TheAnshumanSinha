About

Hey, I'm Anshuman.

Over three decades across talent management, business operations and digital marketing pulled me deep into the startup world, and I never left. As cofounder and CEO of Startup Steroid, I've built a platform that streamlines how investors run their entire investment process.

I've sat through 5,000+ pitches, reviewed more than 10,000 decks, backed 50+ early stage companies, and I manage a $3M fund dedicated to early ventures. Through TiE, from Executive Director and President of TiE SoCal to Chair of TiE SoCal Angels and now the TiE Global Board of Trustees, I've helped build the ecosystem founders lean on.

I also founded Optizm Global, an executive search firm built to assemble high performing teams. Across every role, one thing has stayed constant: quality deliverables and straight, genuinely useful advice.

The process

How we work together.

No contact forms and no cold deck walkthroughs. Four steps from first hello to next steps you can act on the same day.

  1. 01

    Pick your path

    Start with Pitch to me or Talk to me. One runs your raise through a scored review, the other is focused 1:1 time on whatever is in your way.

  2. 02

    Run your ReadyScore or book on Topmate

    Register on the dealflow portal and run your ReadyScore, or book a focused video call on Topmate. Either way, you come prepared.

  3. 03

    We go deep, fast, on the call

    Pitch deck, narrative, valuation, GTM, or the messy decision in front of you. We skip the warmup and get straight to what moves the round.

  4. 04

    You leave with next steps

    Honest, no nonsense next steps you can act on the same day. Clear, specific, and yours to run with.
